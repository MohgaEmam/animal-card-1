White Tiger

The tiger is the biggest species of the cat family .

  • Habitat: habitat depends on the type of food it eats.
  • Diet: will eat a huge range of animals.
  • Life Cycle: will live up to 12-15 years if not caught and killed.
  • Adaptations: adapt with their white body and gray stripes.

White tigers have powerful jaws, razor sharp claws, and huge canine teeth. A full grown white tiger has no other enemy other than humans. These strong animals are at the top of the food chain.
